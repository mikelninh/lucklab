## Cross-cutting reminders

### Reddit — never link-drop

- Always 4+ paragraphs of substance before any URL
- Never post to a sub you have not commented in for at least a week
- Title should be a useful claim, not a question
- Same article = different framing per sub (decidingtobebetter vs GetMotivated vs productivity)

### X — never thread without a hook

- Single posts should stand on their own
- Threads only when the article has 5+ pull-out claims
- Never post 2 single posts on same day on same account
- Reply to every comment in first 3 hours after posting (algo signal)

### Pinterest — pin volume more than pin quality

- 1 pin/day for 60 days > 60 pins on day 1
- Same article can have 3 different pins (same URL, different title/visual)
- Pinterest rewards consistency more than virality
- New pins on existing boards > new boards every week

### Newsletter — once Audience-ID is wired

- One email per week, Sunday
- Format: one short hook (5 lines), one article link, one quiet question
- Never sell directly in the newsletter — sell in the article it links to
- Open rate target: 30%+ (industry standard 22%) → if dropping, the subject lines are the leak
