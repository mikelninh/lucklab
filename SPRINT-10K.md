# Sprint: $10K Month-1 Revenue

> Reverse-engineered from the target. Every decision below serves one number.

## The math

$10,000 = ~€9,300 at current rates.

| Scenario | €9 sales needed | €29 sales needed | Blended (60/40 mix) |
|---|---|---|---|
| All €9 | 1,033 | — | — |
| All €29 | 321 | — | — |
| **60% €9, 40% €29** | **372** | **248** | **620 total sales** |
| **30% €9, 70% €29** | 167 | 248 | **415 total sales** |

At our simulated 1.7% visitor-to-purchase rate: **620 sales needs ~36,500 visitors in month 1.**

That's aggressive. But there's a lever: **improve conversion rate.** If we push from 1.7% to 3% (achievable with a genius funnel), we need ~20,700 visitors. Still big, but one HN front page + one viral X thread + SEO = possible.

**The real play: make the funnel so good that the conversion rate does the heavy lifting, not raw traffic.**

## The plan

Phase 1: Perfect the funnel (today)
Phase 2: Launch (this week)
Phase 3: Amplify (weeks 2-4)

## Phase 1 — The 10/10 funnel

Every touchpoint optimised. No dead ends. Every page moves them forward.

### The visitor journey, step by step:

```
ARRIVE (landing)
  │ ← "Luck is not random. It converges."
  │ ← Daily Kairotic Moment (engagement hook)
  │ ← Reviews rail (social proof)
  │ ← Pricing with clear "Who am I? / Show me more / What's my plan?"
  │
  ▼
EMAIL CAPTURE (Convergence Index)
  │ ← 12,400-word lead magnet — the best free thing they've ever received
  │ ← 5-step drip: T+0 welcome → T+1h nudge → T+24h Wiseman story
  │    → T+3d Primer soft sell → T+7d last touch
  │
  ▼
BEGIN READING (quiz)
  │ ← 10 inputs, auto-advance, progress bar
  │ ← Name after quiz (low-friction)
  │ ← Optional: birthdate + question (with preset chips)
  │
  ▼
FREE RESULT (archetype reveal)
  │ ← Tyche's greeting (by name)
  │ ← One tradition tease
  │ ← LOCKED visual (blurred scores + "your full Reading waits")
  │ ← Two doors: €9 Primer / €29 Full Reading
  │ ← Share button: "Share your archetype" (social card with archetype name)
  │
  ▼
€9 PRIMER (first purchase)
  │ ← Over-deliver: full scores, tradition essay, 7-day practice
  │ ← "Download Your Primer" button (PDF/print)
  │ ← "Share Your Archetype" social card
  │ ← Upsell: "Upgrade to the Full Reading — €20 more"
  │
  ▼
€29 FULL READING (the product)
  │ ← Claude Sonnet 12/10 quality
  │ ← "Download Your Reading" (print-to-PDF with beautiful layout)
  │ ← "Email Me My Reading" button (sends to their inbox)
  │ ← "Share a paragraph" (select text → social card)
  │ ← Gift Reading card: "Send this to someone you love"
  │
  ▼
POST-PURCHASE
  │ ← Day 7: "Did Tyche get it right?" (review request)
  │ ← Day 30: "How's the protocol going?" (re-engagement)
  │ ← Day 90: Return Reading (auto-recalibration)
  │ ← Ongoing: weekly research essays in inbox
```

### What makes this 10/10 vs 7/10:

1. **No dead ends.** Every page has exactly ONE next step. No confusion.
2. **Emotional escalation.** Free = curiosity. €9 = surprise. €29 = recognition. Post = loyalty.
3. **Shareability built into every tier.** Not "share this page" — "share YOUR archetype" (personalised social cards).
4. **Download/email at every paid tier.** The Reading is an OBJECT they keep, not just a webpage.
5. **The Gift Reading is a viral loop**, not a feature. Each buyer potentially creates a new buyer.
